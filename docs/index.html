ALCHEMY

A PegNet Protocol Client

for interaction with the Factom blockchain's network of stablecoins

Mining for PNT

Oracles fetch asset conversion rates and mine for high difficulty submissions.

Rewards per Block

The top 10 graded submissions are rewarded with PNT.

Filtered by PoW

The top 50 submissions are by Proof-of-Work are eligible to compete in the block.

Conversions

Convert between any two assets in place. No second or third parties.

Grading

The submissions are graded against each other and ranked.

Tx Fees < $0.0012

Factom Entries can hold 0 to 10 KB and cost $0.001 to $0.01. Many transactions can fit in one entry.
